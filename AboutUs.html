About Us!

What is NZSALE

NZSALE is New Zealand’s exclusive members-only online shopping club and your doorway to affordable designer fashion. NZSALE (www.nzsale.co.nz) is a website operated by OZSALE Pty Limited (ABN 11 118 610 987), a company registered in Australia. Each day, members receive a personal invitation to the most exclusive sales events for men, women, kids, home, beauty, wine and more – at up to 80% off retail. Membership is free and everyone is welcome to join!

Who are NZSALE

NZSALE has a dedicated team of buyers located in Australia, the USA, Europe, Asia and New Zealand who work hard to find our customers the best deals possible. With a range of support staff all over the world – we will endeavour to make the best online shopping experience for you!

Why Members-only?

  • By being an exclusive shopping club, NZSALE is able to offer products you cannot find anywhere else. With stock being limited, products are reserved exclusively for NZSALE members.

How do I invite my friends to join:

Simply click on the Iink located at the top of the homepage to access your personal invitation screen. From here – you can invite a friend directly, send them a link – or share via social media!
